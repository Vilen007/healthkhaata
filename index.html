"How it works"

Use Desktop or Laptop

Use Laptop or Desktop This program can only be used on a laptop or desktop compute, if necessary, we may later produce a mobile version.

Upload the reports


Upload the two reports you want to compare. We advise uploading the older report as Report 1 and the most recent report as Report 2. Thus, when comparing results, the most recent findings will always be on the right and the older ones on the left.

Compare

Click Check Result once you've chosen your reports. It can take upto two minutes to process the findings, so Kindly be patient

While you wait the reports are being checked for the tests indicated under the "All available Tests" tab so that the data can be compared.

The Results

Results are shown in two tables

Matched results displays the tests that are present in both reports; the data for same pararneters is displayed next to one another, making comparisons easy for users.

All Results displays all result from both reports, which include matching and non-matching parameters. The results aren't displayed next to one another in this case

It's important to know that neither tab will show tests that aren't mentioned under the "All available tests" tab
